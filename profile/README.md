We tokenize income-producing properties so anyone can own real estate starting at $50, collect monthly rent distributions proportional to their holdings, and trade property tokens on a secondary market — all on-chain, all auditable.

Not a yield product. Not DeFi real estate. Real property, real returns, variable and transparent.

---

### How it works

1. **Property onboarded** — Real property is acquired, structured in an SPV, and tokenized via audited smart contracts
2. **Investors buy tokens** — KYC-verified investors purchase fractional ownership through an initial property sale
3. **Rent flows on-chain** — Monthly rental income is distributed pro-rata to all token holders
4. **Trade anytime** — Property tokens trade on SecondaryDAO's secondary market with on-chain settlement
